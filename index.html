House Building

Taylor Lane Timber Frame Ltd is one of the UK’s leading timber frame construction specialists. For over 40 years, we have designed and manufactured timber frame systems for a wide variety of sectors including self-build, private and affordable housing, education, care homes and student accommodation. Combining knowledge and expertise with the latest design software and manufacturing equipment, we enable our clients to build efficient, high-quality homes, faster. Our timber frame presents a speedy, cost-effective and energy-efficient offsite construction method.
