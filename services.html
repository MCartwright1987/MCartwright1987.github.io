OUR SERVICES

SERVICE

Preventive maintenance is, without a doubt, the most effective way to reduce downtime. It's during these maintenance routines that we find potential costly breakdowns. By finding these early, we can take the right steps to prevent failure, reduce costs, preserve the life of your machine & keep you in full production.

RECOMISSION

With meticulous precision and dedicated care, our team specializes in the comprehensive assessment, refurbishment, and recalibration of CNC equipment. Our commitment is to revive your machinery to its optimal performance, ensuring it operates seamlessly, just like when it was brand new.

BREAKDOWNS

Recognizing the urgency during breakdowns, our team, equipped with relevant skills, manufacturer training, and a well-organized setup, is committed to swift and efficient support. With strategic spare parts and advanced tools, we minimize downtime, ensuring your operations resume seamlessly.

DECOMISSION

When it's time to retire or relocate your equipment, our experienced team handles every step meticulously. From disassembly to proper disposal, we ensure environmentally responsible practices while maximising the salvageable value of components.

SPARES

When unexpected glitches occur, our skilled technicians are on standby to diagnose and address issues. With a vast inventory of high-quality spare parts, we ensure minimal downtime and rapid restoration of your CNC operations.
